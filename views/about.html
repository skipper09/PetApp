About the Dallas Animal Shelter

Our founder, Sarah, with her first rescue in 1973.

The Dallas Animal Shelter was founded in the garage of one pet lover's home as a rescue operation. As the volume of rescued animals increased, so did the need for a place to house these animals, and so the Dallas Animal Shelter was created.

The Dallas Animal Shelter has been in operation since 1973.
